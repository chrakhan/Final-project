Popular Attractions

Felaw Pond

Felaw pond is one of the most beautiful resorts in Iraqi Kurdistan. The pond is one of the high level and beautiful natural ponds in Kurdistan as well.

Gali Sherena Valley

Gali Sherana is a beautiful blue river located in the town of Derelok in Duhok province. Due to its crystal clear waters, It has been one of the most beautiful and attractive places in Kurdistan.

Rawanduz Canyon

The Canyon is located in the northern part of Erbil province, in Town of Rawanduz. The Canyon is one of the most beautiful places in Kurdistan. The canyon is filled with lush greenery and natural rock formations that are perfect for hiking and exploring.
